The Game Changer

Nick Mitchell is the trailblazing health & fitness industry entrepreneur who redefined the modern personal training industry. The only Founder to ever make a notable private equity exit within the 1-2-1 personal training space.

"I consider U.P. to have been my warm-up. The undergrad degree is done, now it's time to kick it up a gear!"

As the brains behind the entirety of the Ultimate Performance (U.P.) offering, Nick Mitchell built a global personal training business that was previously both unthinkable and unbackable in a sector that no one had managed to scale before, during, or, to date, after him.

His entrepreneurial journey began with a single basement gym in the City of London and a vision to redefine the personal training industry. From scratch, growing through profit, not debt, cash flow, or external investment, he built Ultimate Performance into a global powerhouse. With over 30 locations across 4 continents and a reputation as the gold standard in results-driven, career-focused fitness, what resulted was the widely acknowledged industry leader and a truly unique business that changed the game in the sector, culminating in a majority sale to one of the UK's leading private equity houses, Inflexion.

Through a relentless focus on innovation, customer results, and building a world-class team, he achieved what many thought was impossible: scaling a premium, personal training business into a global brand and ultimately achieving an exit that is unprecedented in the personal training industry.
